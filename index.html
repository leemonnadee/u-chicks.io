    • OUR STORY

    The inspiration for our business concept struck during a memorable dining experience at our favorite Korean restaurant. As we savored each bite of the dishes served, the idea began to take shape – why not introduce a unique fusion of flavors that pays homage to Korean cuisine while offering something new and exciting to our community? It was during this moment that the concept of U-Chicks, short for "University Chicken," was born. We wanted to create a dining experience that catered to the vibrant energy and diverse tastes of university vibes.

    About me

    The Team

    Meet Rain Grace San Jose, the visionary behind U-Chicks. Currently in her third year as an entrepreneurship student at the University of Santo Tomas, Rain embodies the spirit of innovation and passion for culinary excellence.

    Having grown up in Korea, Rain developed a deep appreciation for the country's rich culture and, most notably, its mouthwatering cuisine. Her fond memories of savoring authentic Korean flavors inspired her to share the culinary delights of Korea with her fellow Filipinos.

    Driven by her love for Korea and her entrepreneurial spirit, Rain embarked on a journey to bring the vibrant and flavorful experience of Korean-inspired chicken dishes to the Philippines. With a relentless dedication to quality and a commitment to delivering unforgettable dining experiences, Rain is proud to introduce U-Chicks as a culinary destination where bold flavors and innovative concepts collide.
